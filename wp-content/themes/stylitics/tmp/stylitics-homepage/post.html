3 Perfect Weekend Looks

2

January is National Organization Month, and what better place to start than with your closet? We showed you how to get started, but now comes the painful part: getting rid of stuff. I don’t know about you, but I have a tendency to develop a deep-seated personal connection with my clothes the second I try to give them away. Here are some resources to help you figure out if it really is time to part ways:

Check out this article for great tips on weeding out stuff you don’t need. (She Knows)

Wondering which trends are going to translate in the New Year? Looks like it might be time to get rid of the mullet skirt, the crop top, and ombre, among other things. (Inquirer)

And even though Anne Hathaway rocked a couture peplum at the Golden Globes last night, this article says they might be on their way out as well… (GMA News)

And don’t worry, guys, we didn’t forget you! Check out the top 5 menswear trends of 2013, so you don’t accidentally throw away a gem. (Fashionising)

Good luck, and happy organizing! What will you be throwing away this year?
